100xEngineers is a course provider offering a generative AI engineering course.  The course focuses on practical skills rather than just tools like ChatGPT.  It emphasizes project-based learning and covers various topics including:

* **Fundamentals:** Generative AI history, research, and platforms.
* **Diffusion Models:** Detailed explanations and application, including Stable Diffusion.
* **Prompt Engineering:** Techniques for effective prompt creation.
* **Fine-tuning Models:**  Using LoRA datasets, parameters, and advanced techniques.
* **Model Deployment (MLOps):** Implementation and deployment of models.
* **PyTorch, LLMs, and AI Agents:**  Understanding neural networks, large language models, and hands-on experience with AI agents like Autogen and crewAI.
* **RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation):** Usage of Langchain and Llamaindex.
* **Vector Databases:**  Leveraging vector databases for LLM memory.
* **Tools & Frameworks:**  Practical application of Python, Langchain, GitHub, Google Colab, DALL-E 3, PyTorch, Hugging Face, CivitAI, Midjourney, Replicate, Dreambooth, OpenAI API, Auto1111, Stable Diffusion, and ComfyUI.

The course is intended for beginners and experienced professionals wanting to upskill or transition careers.  The course creators include experienced AI professionals and educators.
